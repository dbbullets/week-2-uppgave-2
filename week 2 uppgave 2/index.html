Bunnies

Known as a rabbit, is a small mammal with long ears, soft fur, and a short fluffy tail. Rabbits are known for their strong hind legs, which help them hop quickly and escape predators. They are herbivores, mainly eating grass, vegetables, and hay. Bunnies are social and gentle animals, often kept as pets because of their calm nature and cute appearance.

Hyraxes

Hyrax is a small, herbivorous mammal that looks a bit like a large guinea pig, but it is more closely related to elephants and manatees. Hyraxes are native to Africa and the Middle East and often live in rocky areas or cliffs. They have short tails, rounded ears, and thick fur. Despite their small size, they are social animals that live in groups and communicate with a variety of sounds.

Cats

A cat is a small, domesticated mammal known for its independence, agility, and playful nature. Cats belong to the species Felis catus and are popular pets around the world. They have sharp retractable claws, excellent night vision, and strong hunting instincts. Cats communicate through meowing, purring, body language, and facial expressions. Many people keep cats as companions because they are affectionate, relatively low-maintenance, and adaptable to different living environments.
